Selected Work

Six products, elegantly structured.

From dense enterprise dashboards to seamless consumer fintech — explore how I turn complex problems into highly functional interfaces.

Project 01

Neo CFO

Enterprise FinTech Dashboard

A complete UI/UX overhaul of an enterprise financial platform used by finance teams to manage complex reporting, forecasting, and analysis workflows.

Problem

Finance teams worked across fragmented tools. Getting a clear read on numbers took far longer than it should.

Approach

Restructured the architecture around how teams actually think, designing a clean dashboard built for dense data.

Outcome

A cohesive platform where forecasting and analysis feel structured, breathable, and highly navigable.

Impact

Teams moved from raw data to decisions in far fewer steps. The UI became the standard for future products.

Project 02

F Index Software

Product Design Design Systems

End-to-end product design for F Index Software, from early research through to a shipped, scalable design system.

Problem

The product needed a design foundation that could scale fast, while features kept shipping on a live roadmap.

Approach

Ran user research, mapped wireframes, and crafted a full UI language that mapped directly to engineering builds.

Outcome

A consistent design system that let the team design and build faster, with minimal duplicated effort.

Impact

New features could be designed against a robust system, drastically shortening the concept-to-release cycle.

Project 03

Spark AI

AI Product Interface Design

Interface design for Spark AI, a platform built around AI-assisted workflows and prompt-based interactions.

Problem

AI features only work if people trust them. Prompting needed to feel natural, not like operating a black box.

Approach

Designed the experience around clarity, transparency, and structure, giving users control over AI generated results.

Outcome

A modern, focused interface where AI-assisted workflows are incredibly fast to start and easy to trust.

Impact

Users complete tasks with zero friction, and these interaction patterns became the company's gold standard.
